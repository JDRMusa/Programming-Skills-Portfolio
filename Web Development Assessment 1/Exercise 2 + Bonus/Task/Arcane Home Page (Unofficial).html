ARCANE


Welcome to the world of Runeterra, a world of magic and technology, war and tranquility, heroes and villains. This is how the events of this world shape and mould the current day of Runeterra. The Arcane shows the past that results the present, and other realities that it brought.

A Netflix series made by the game company Riot Games and the French animation company Fortiche, was a hit among fans and watchers because of the compelling and intricate story that allows people to get hooked right away. It also shows how the animated series explains contexts and plots that do not need them to play the game the story is based on. They are planning to make a third season exploring other continents of the world of Runeterra, where the story takes place.

The first and second seasons occur in the twin cities called Piltover and Zaun. Piltover is a place of technological progress, where people from different cities gather and trade for goods. The place is full of life as the sky is seen from its tall buildings. It is a stark comparison against its other twin, Zaun. Zaun is an underground city, where morals are gray and hardships are born with. The city is full of pollutants that makes the quality of life contrasts those who are above them.


Characters


Jinx - Jinx, born Powder, is one of the main protagonists in Arcane. She is the unpredictable, ingenious, and chaotic little sister of Vi and adoptive daughter to Silco. Throughout the show she is disturbed with feelings of abandonment from her sister, along with the manipulation she faced from Silco as she tries to determine what her true sense of self is.

Violet - (commonly known as Vi) is one of the main protagonists of Arcane. She is the strong-willed, determined, family oriented, and short-tempered older sister to Jinx and adoptive daughter of Vander. She spends the series struggling between her loyalty to Zaun and her family to her lover Caitlyn Kiramman and her sister's new form as Jinx.

Caitlyn Kiramman - She is the skilled, determined, and justice-seeking daughter of Cassandra Kiramman and Tobias Kiramman, and therefore a member of the wealthy and influential House Kiramman. C aitlyn dedicated herself to uncovering Silco's empire in the undercity with the help of Vi. During the first act of Season 2, she gets overwhelmed by her hatred of Jinx for killing her mother, compared to her original goal of helping the people of the undercity.

Mel Medarda - She is a main character in Arcane. An intelligent, powerful, and ambitious heiress of House Medarda, she was councilor of Piltover and mage. She was a patroness of Hextech and Jayce Talis' lover, but now returned to her home of Noxus after she had battled her mother Ambessa Medarda and won.

Jayce Talis - He was a main character in Arcane. An ambitious, intelligent, and compassionate man, he and his mother were saved by arcane magic and he became dedicated to finding a way to harness this magic. By becoming partners with the Zaunite inventor Viktor, they successfully achieve his goal in creating Hextech. Jayce is later appointed councillor and supported by his lover, Mel Medarda, but struggles between these new responsibilities and his and Viktor's dream of saving lives.

Viktor - (also known as the Herald or the Machine Herald) was a main character of Arcane. Ingenious and courageous, he came from the Undercity to become an inventor and created Hextech alongside his partner, Jayce Talis. As his health worsens he struggles with a desperation to find cure by using Shimmer and studying the Hexcore, which eventually causes him to become the Machine Herald and lead a cult.

Silco - He was the main antagonist in Season 1 and a posthumous character in Season 2 of Arcane, and he was the second adopted father of Jinx. He was also the powerful, ambitious Zaunite leader of the chem-barons, silently controlling Zaun from under the noses of Piltover's authority.

Ambessa Medarda - She was a supporting character in Arcane. She was a manipulative, calculating, ruthless, and powerful warlord from Noxus. Ambessa thought of her family as the most important part of her life and was determined to secure her legacy.

Vander - (later known as Warwick) was a supporting character in Arcane. He was once the determined and zealous unspoken leader and protector of the undercity. Vander did all he could in his later life to ensure the safety of those under his protection—the residents of Zaun and, more specifically, the four children he'd taken under his wing, as his own. When a series of events forces him to choose between an established truce between Zaun and Piltover and his children, Vander's life is up-ended by his past.

Ekko - He is a major character appearing in Arcane. One of many children who grew up in the Lanes. Upon the death of his adoptive father and the disappearance of his friends, Ekko finds a way to survive on his own and help others fight back against Silco's reign over their home.


Summary


It starts with the two sisters, orphaned to a barkeep named Vander. Being a Zaunite, the sisters and their friends went for a heist, looting a residence that was tipped by the 'little guy'. Then one action made their life change forever as a magical artifact that the younger sister tried to steal struck the ground.

The topsiders they call Pilties knew about the incident and decided to take action, making their military force hunt in the underbelly of Zaun. To protect the sisters, Vander, decided to sacrifice himself which is found out by Violet, the older sister. The then tried to take his place. The plan failed as the chem-baron, Silco, decided to ambush both the barkeep and the police force. He sucessfully captured Vander and eliminated the piltover force. The sisters determined to get their foster father back, they tried to sneak in and free him. But due to differences, they failed and things got much worse.

Years pass by, the sisters grew up in different situations, separated by their actions. Powder developed a persona of mayhem and destruction, named Jinx. Its the same word Vi (Violet) called her before they split up, and with this persona, she wrecked havoc as the left hand man of Silco. Violet, taken in to prison by the remaining Piltovan officer, was released by Caitlyn Kiramman. Caitlyn is an officer who tracks down Jinx, who made mayhem and chaos on the topside and tries to bring her to justice. But her only lead is Vi.

Along this, Caitlyn's friend - Jayce Talos, is developing a new technology where people with no magical ability could harness magic. He is inspired by his past for he is saved by a wizard that handed him a magical rune. With his friend and partner, Viktor who has a disease that made him cripple, tries to do it in secret, but they are caught by Mel Medarda. Mel, even though a council member, saw a bright future with them and decided to keep it a secret, hence them succeeding and creating Hextech. Heimerdinger, a small Yordle professor and council member, was astonished as he saw what was happening and gave partial approval to their works.
